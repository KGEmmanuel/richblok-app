Introduction

RichBlok Inc. operates https://www.richblok.com, a professional network of trust. RichBlok’s privacy policy applies to all users of our websites, apps and its services. RichBlok’s registered users, called “Member”, use our services whereas none registered users, called “Visitor” can view some of our services.

Each Member can certify all contents in the web with our technology. Precisely, a Member records professional statements and interacts with the network to certify these statements and associated documents. A Member demonstrate recorded statements and can take training to improve their skills and knowledge. Professional evaluations offered within RichBlok establish “current knowledge and skills base”.

This privacy policy will explain how our organization uses the personal data we collect from you when you use our website.

1. What data do we collect?

A. Registration

When you need to create RichBlok account, you have to provide the following data:

  • Email address
  • Mobile number
  • Name
  • Password
  • Credit card information to buy a premium account or premium services.

B. Records

RichBlok enables you to records any information that value you in the job market. Therefore, you can record statements such as education, skills, diplomas, transcripts, work experience, photo, work attestation, city or area, certificates, experiences, contracts, tests results, performances…etc. These records enable you use RichBlok services such as certification, demonstration, training, evaluation, job application, payments, investment, payments, advertising, and selling. It is your choice whether to record sensitive information on your profile and to make that sensitive information public. Please do not post or add personal data to your profile that you would not want to be publicly available.

C. Background Checks

People in the RichBlok network can certify information you record and give their opinions about your statements in order to produce background checks for employments. While these opinions and certifications are bind to your profile with a legal value, you have the right to contest them. Background checks are performed only if you approve. So, do not approve background checks that are unauthorized in your country or region.

D. Service User

While you are on RichBlok, registered or visitor, we log your service usage. Data usage we log include the use of our services found in our website, platform technology, mobile apps, RichBlok certified contents, ads, search, publications or job application. We use log-ins, cookies, device information and internet protocol (“IP”) addresses to identify you and log your use.

E. Cookies

Date we collect come from cookies and similar technologies. As described in RichBlok’s Cookie Policy, we use cookies and similar technologies (e.g., web beacons, pixels, ad tags and device identifiers) to recognize you and/or your device(s) on, off and across different services and devices. We also allow some others to use cookies as described in our Cookie Policy. You can control cookies through your browser settings and other tools. You can also opt-out from our use of cookies and similar technologies that track your behavior on the sites of others for third party advertising.

F. Your device and location

Our trusted network demand the use of data from your devices and networks, including location data. When you visit or leave our services (including our plugins or cookies or similar technology on the sites of others), we receive the URL of both the site you came from and the one you go to next. If you use our services from a mobile device, that device will send us data about your location based on your phone settings. 7. Your Choices & Obligations

A. Data retention

We keep most of your personal data for as long as your account is open. We retain your personal data while your account is in existence or as needed to provide you services. This includes data you or others provided to us and data generated or inferred from your use of our services. Even if you only use our services when looking for a new job every few years, we will retain your information and keep your profile open until you decide to close your account. In some cases we choose to retain certain information in a depersonalized or aggregated form.

B. Rights to Access and Control Your Personal Data

You can access or delete your personal data. You have many choices about how your data is collected, used and shared. We provide many choices about the collection, use and sharing of your data, from deleting or correcting data you include in your profile and controlling the visibility of your posts to advertising opt-outs and communication controls. We offer you settings to control and manage the personal data we have about you. For personal data that we have about you:

  • Delete Data: You can ask us to erase or delete all or some of your personal data (e.g., if it is no longer necessary to provide services to you).
  • Change or Correct Data: You can edit some of your personal data through your account. You can also ask us to change, update or fix your data in certain cases, particularly if it’s inaccurate.
  • Object to, or Limit or Restrict, Use of Data: You can ask us to stop using all or some of your personal data (e.g., if we have no legal right to keep using it) or to limit our use of it (e.g., if your personal data is inaccurate or unlawfully held).
  • Right to Access and/or Take Your Data: You can ask us for a copy of your personal data and can ask for a copy of personal data you provided in machine-readable form.

Visitors can learn more about how to make these requests here. You may also contact us using the contact information below, and we will consider your request in accordance with applicable laws. Residents in the European Union or European Economic Area may have additional rights under their laws.

B. Account Closure

We keep some of your data even after you close your account. If you choose to close your RichBlok account, your personal data will generally stop being visible to others on our services within 24 hours. We generally delete closed account information within 30 days of account closure, except as noted below.

We retain your personal data even after you have closed your account if reasonably necessary to comply with our legal obligations (including law enforcement requests), meet regulatory requirements, resolve disputes, maintain security, prevent fraud and abuse, enforce our User Agreement, or fulfill your request to “unsubscribe” from further messages from us. We will retain de-personalized information after your account has been closed.

Information you have shared with others will remain visible after you closed your account or deleted the information from your own profile or mailbox, and we do not control data that other Members copied out of our services. Groups content and ratings or review content associated with closed accounts will show an unknown user as the source. Your profile may continue to be displayed in the services of others (e.g., search engine results) until they refresh their cache.
