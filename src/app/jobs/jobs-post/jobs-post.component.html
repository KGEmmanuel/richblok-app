RichBlok Application Tracking Software interface

A 5 step recruitment process
Step 1

Job offer

Welcome on the RichBlok tracking software interface. In this step your are required to post the job offer, define pre-selection criteria, pre-interviews questionnaires and challenges.


This step must be completed before going to the next step

Step 2

Sorting

In this step our AI will authomatically sort the top between the applicants and propose them to you. You will be able to prepare the pre-interview, check their background and send some to the pre-interviews


This step must be completed before going to the next step

Step 3

Scoring

You are on the right way. This step requires you to do the scoring of the pre-interviews for each participant, formulate the salary package and invitation to the final interviews


This step must be completed before going to the next step

Step 4

Purposes

You are almost done. You are mainly required here to do the final interviews and transmission of the process report



This step must be completed before going to the next step

Step 5

Recruit

Final Step. Here you just have to send the certified recruitment notes and smart contract to the selected candidates
